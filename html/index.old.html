About Aveade Point of Sale

Aveade Point of Sale (POS) is a powerful, integrated software solution designed to empower brick-and-mortar and multi-channel businesses to manage in-store sales efficiently and effortlessly. Built by Aveade to complement our digital platform, our POS system is the central nervous system for your physical retail operations. We believe that technology should simplify, not complicate. Aveade POS is focused on providing a seamless, single platform experience, helping you unify your inventory, sales, and customer data across your physical store and your existing Aveade online channels.At Aveade, our goal is to build inclusive technology that reduces the cost and complexity of running a small to medium-sized business. Aveade Point of Sale is a testament to this commitment, offering a reliable, integrated, and feature-rich solution that lets you focus on what matters most: serving your customers and growing your business.

The Following Features are available at no additional cost, unlimited multiple locations and users, print prices, print barcodes, print invoices, print packing slip, unlimited data access, opening balance, closing balance, guest checkout, integration and access to API, unlimited staff profiles, user roles and permissions, free support, flexible plans, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime, works with all different hardware including mobile devices, tablets with online and offline capabilities, inventory management, Receive automatic low stock alerts

staff management, logs, barcodes generating, AI search by voice, image, integration with online Aveade marketplace, peripharials supported by WiFi, or connected to hardware, opening balance, closing balance, sales reports, manage user profile, print slip, QR Code scan, Cart holding to avoid void and long queues, self checkout, Manage and set different pricing for different store locations, assign products for stores,customer can check out on their own without a cashier in store, add exsisting or new barcode, front and back camera for tablets, notification sounds, scanning sound, refund, security include password, pincode, and finger print scanner. Built in payments include card payment with Aveade Pay, Cash payment, Slipt payment with card and cash, bank transfer, search function, backoff include branding, you can put your logo or customise thank you message on your slip, email marketing for customers who provided their emails, access control, access online orders settings, discounts and tips, language and currency of your choice.
